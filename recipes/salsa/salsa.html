Salsa

Description

Salsa or Pico de Gallo, fresh and tangy. Perfect side to any mexican dish.

Ingredients

  • Red onion
  • Jalapeno pepper
  • Limes
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop onion, jalapeno pepper, tomatoes and cilantro
  2. Mix onion jalapeno pepper and lime juice in a bowl
  3. Allow to stand for 5 minutes
  4. Mix in tomatoes and cilantro
  5. Add salt to taste
  6. Allow to stand for 15 more minutes for flavours to blend
  7. Enjoy
