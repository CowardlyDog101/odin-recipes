Tzatziki

Description

Tzatziki is a dip, simple and delicious. Perfect for pitas, grilled veggies or meat, sandwiches and more.

Ingredients

  • Greek Yoghurt
  • Cucumber
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mince garlic
  2. Grate cucumber
  3. Add minced garlic, grated cucumber and yogurt to a bowl
  4. Mix garlic,cucumber and yogurt
  5. Add salt to taste
  6. Allow to stand for flavours to blend
  7. Enjoy
